ABOUT US

BuzyBus aims at making public transportation efficient & more convinient in the state of Himachal Pradesh. We Utilize Technolgies such as GPS, to update the Real-Time Location of the buses whilst incorporating the traffic management system so as to provide optimized time of arrival & departure.

What We Offer

What do we provide?

  • Provides live location.
  • Regulary updated time schedule of buses.
  • Provides Information of nearest basic ammenities &
    sustainibility information.

Why Choose Us?

  • Better provision of security & disaster manangement.
  • We use Real-Time location i.e accurate and optimized time solution.
  • In case of unavailibility of network we provide accurate response based
    on pre-fed time schedule.
